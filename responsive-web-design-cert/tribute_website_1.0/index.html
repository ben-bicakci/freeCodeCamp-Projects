Valentino Rossi

A fan page for MotoGP legend Valentino Rossi

#VR46

Valentino Rossi, born 16 February 1979, is an Italian professional motorcycle road racer and multiple MotoGP World Champion. He is the only rider in the history of the World Championship to have won the World Championship in four different classes: 125cc (1), 250cc (1), 500cc (1) and MotoGP (6). Rossi is one of the most successful motorcycle racers of all time, with nine Grand Prix World Championships to his name â€“ seven of which are in the premier class.

Rossi won the 500cc World Championship with Honda in 2001, the MotoGP Championships in 2002 and 2003 with Honda, and again in 2004 and 2005 with Yamaha. Rossi regained the title in 2008 and retained it in 2009. In 2010, after a broken leg and no title challenge, Rossi left Yamaha to for Ducati. He rejoined Yamaha in 2013 and 2014 after two unsuccesful seasons with Ducati. He finished second in 2014, 2015 and 2016, and is contracted to race until the end of the 2020 season (he will be 41 years old).
